# Sales Performance & Marketing Spend Analytics

## Project Overview
An advanced Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze and evaluate corporate sales revenues in direct relation to monthly marketing investments. This interactive dashboard assists key stakeholders and the sales team in identifying high-performing regions, evaluating team contributions, and optimizing marketing budget distribution to drive conversion rates.

## Key Features & Insights
* **Comprehensive Executive Metrics:** Monitored over **$1.04M** in total revenue with a deep dive into average transactional metrics and execution tracking across 16 primary corporate orders.
* **Regional Performance & Ranking:** Structured an intuitive geo-performance area visual to rank organizational sales impact across regions (East, North, South, West).
* **Marketing Spend Optimization:** Visualized a detailed monthly breakdown comparing marketing spend streams against gross generated revenue to calculate exact business ROI.
* **Waterfall Variance Analysis:** Implemented a Waterfall chart tracking positive and negative changes in marketing allocation across strategic operational channels (Team A, Team B).

## Tools & Technologies Used
* **Data Preparation & Modeling:** Microsoft Excel (Data Source) and Power Query for automated ETL pipeline construction and cleaning.
* **Data Analysis & Calculations:** Power BI Platform utilizing calculated columns and core aggregation metrics.
* **Interactive Visualization:** Power BI Service for cloud collaboration and distribution.
